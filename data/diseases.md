# Disease Information Database

## 1. Rubella (German Measles)

**Name:** Rubella  
**Thai Name:** หัดเยอรมัน  
**Cause:** Rubella virus (Togaviridae family)

**Symptoms:**
- Low-grade fever (37.5-38.5°C)
- Pink rash starting from face
- Swollen lymph nodes behind ears and neck
- Headache and fatigue

**Treatment:**
- Rest at home
- Paracetamol for fever
- Calamine lotion for itching
- Plenty of fluids

**Prevention:**
- MMR vaccine (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)
- First dose at 9-12 months
- Second dose at 4-6 years

## 2. Cholera

**Name:** Cholera  
**Thai Name:** อหิวาตกโรค  
**Cause:** Vibrio cholerae bacteria

**Symptoms:**
- Severe watery diarrhea
- Vomiting
- Rapid dehydration
- Muscle cramps

**Treatment:**
- Oral rehydration therapy (ORT)
- Intravenous fluids if severe
- Antibiotics in severe cases
- Zinc supplements

**Prevention:**
- Clean drinking water
- Proper sanitation
- Hand washing
- Oral cholera vaccine

## 3. Cataract

**Name:** Cataract  
**Thai Name:** ต้อกระจก  
**Cause:** Aging, UV exposure, diabetes

**Symptoms:**
- Cloudy or blurry vision
- Difficulty seeing at night
- Sensitivity to light
- Fading colors

**Treatment:**
- Surgery (only treatment)
- Phacoemulsification
- Intraocular lens implant

**Prevention:**
- Wear sunglasses (UV protection)
- Control diabetes
- Quit smoking
- Regular eye exams

---

## 4. GERD (Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease)

**Name:** GERD  
**Thai Name:** กรดไหลย้อน  
**Cause:** Weakened lower esophageal sphincter

**Symptoms:**
- Heartburn
- Acid regurgitation
- Difficulty swallowing
- Chest pain

**Treatment:**
- Antacids
- H2 blockers
- Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs)
- Lifestyle changes

**Prevention:**
- Avoid large meals
- Don't lie down after eating
- Maintain healthy weight
- Avoid trigger foods (spicy, fatty)
